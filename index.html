    About Us

    Unique Furniture Masters (UFM) for home and decor was established in 2019 by Mr. Sibusiso Masondo with the carpentry background and Mr. Maqhawe Mpanza with the engineering background.

    Unique Furniture Masters (UFM) /are the designers, manufactures and installers of kitchen units, bedroom cupboards, beds, headboards, sideboard, TV stands, vanities and many more. We believe in top quality and elegant furniture..

    We comply with valued design and architectural principles to ensure that high levels of comfort and customer satisfactory is achieved. Our skilled and talented staffs are trained to scrupulously interpret customer requirements and convert them to prestigious final products to ensure that satisfaction is guaranteed. We currently operating but not limited to two Provinces Gauteng and Kwa-Zulu Natal Province. To turn your house into a home contact us we are just a telephone call away.
